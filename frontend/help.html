Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a skill listing?
To create a skill listing, go to your profile and click on "Add Skill". Fill in the details about what you can offer and what you're looking for in return. Be specific to attract the right swap partners!
Is SkillSwap safe to use?
Yes! We have several safety features including user verification, ratings and reviews, and in-app messaging. Always meet in public places for the first time and trust your instincts.
What happens if my swap partner doesn't show up?
If your partner doesn't show up, you can report it through the app. After verification, we'll help you find another partner and may restrict the no-show user's account.
Can I swap skills virtually?
Absolutely! Many skills can be swapped virtually. When creating your listing, you can specify whether you're offering in-person, virtual, or both types of swaps.
